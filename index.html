Our Story

Special Treat Bakery is a family‑run artisan takeaway in Scone, Perth. Drop in for fresh sourdough loaves, handcrafted pastries, indulgent traybakes, and beautifully made celebration cakes, along with a tempting rotation of daily specials. Enjoy your treats with our full range of Nespresso coffees, espresso, americano, flat white, cappuccino, and latte, or choose from our comforting selection of teas.
